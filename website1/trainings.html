YOGA

Yoga is a great addition to any fitness routine. Unlike your high-intensity interval training (HIIT) workouts or weight-lifting sessions, yoga is often slow, and it focuses more on being mindful and stretching out the body—two things that come with huge benefits. It's totally possible, though, to incorporate strength training into a yoga workout too.

WEIGHT LIFTING

Weight training is a common type of strength training for developing the strength and size of skeletal muscles. It uses the force of gravity in the form of dumbbells in order to oppose the force generated by muscle through concentric or eccentric contraction. Weight training uses a variety of specialized equipment to target specific muscle.

ABS

Abs makes your body fit. It’s surprising how many people miss the abdominal muscles when working out. In doing so, they miss out on the numerous benefits of abdominal workouts.Abdominal muscles play a crucial role in posture, support of the spine, balance, stability, and respiratory functions such as breathing.
